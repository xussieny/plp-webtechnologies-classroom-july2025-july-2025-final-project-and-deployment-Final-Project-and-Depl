About Visit Ethiopia

VisitEthiopia is a platform dedicated to helping travelers discover amazing destinations in Ethiopia. Founded in 2025, it helps travelers plan their dream vacations.

Our mission is to inspire and enable people to visit Ethiopian historical places responsibly and authentically. We believe that travel has the power to broaden perspectives, create connections, and foster understanding between cultures.
